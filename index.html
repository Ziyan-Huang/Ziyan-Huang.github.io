About

  • I am a Young Researcher at the Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory.
  • I received my Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and my B.Eng. in Biomedical Engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University.
  • My research interests include medical image analysis, multimodal foundation models for medicine, and medical AI agents.
  • I have published multiple papers in leading journals and conferences, including Medical Image Analysis (MedIA) and IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics (JBHI), and have won multiple championships in MICCAI international medical image analysis challenges.
