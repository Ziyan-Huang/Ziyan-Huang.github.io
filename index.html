About

  • I am a Researcher at Shanghai AI Laboratory and a member of General Medical AI (GMAI).
  • I received my Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University supervised by Prof. Lixu Gu, and my B.Eng. in Biomedical Engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University.
  • My research interests include medical image analysis, medical multimodal foundation models, and medical AI agents.
  • I have won multiple championships in MICCAI challenges.
