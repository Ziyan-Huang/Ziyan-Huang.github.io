About

  • I am a Researcher at Shanghai AI Laboratory and a member of General Medical AI (GMAI).
  • I received my Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and my B.Eng. in Biomedical Engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University.
  • My research interests include medical image analysis, medical multimodal foundation models, and medical AI agents.
  • I have published papers in journals and conferences, including Medical Image Analysis (MedIA) and IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics (JBHI), and have won multiple championships in MICCAI international medical image analysis challenges.
